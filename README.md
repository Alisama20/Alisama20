## About me

I am a master's student specialising in the numerical simulation of physical systems. My work focuses on:

- **Kinetic theory of plasmas** — Vlasov–Maxwell equations, semi-Lagrangian schemes, Particle-in-Cell methods
- **Computational electromagnetics** — Method of Moments (EFIE/MFIE), scattering by PEC bodies
- **Monte Carlo methods** — Diffusion Monte Carlo for quantum systems, stochastic simulation
- **Spectral and finite-difference methods** — FFT, Chebyshev–Galerkin, Crank–Nicolson
